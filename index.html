Bio

I am a PhD student in the CS Department at UIUC. I am advised by Luke Olson and additionally work closely with Bill Gropp and Amanda Bienz.

My research interests lie in the intersection of numerical algorithms and high performance computing, focusing on the design of scalable iterative solvers for emerging supercomputer architectures.

Here is my Curriculum Vitae.
